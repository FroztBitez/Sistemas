ABOUT THEM

I'll watch...watch as you destroy all you've created...watch as everything, every single speck of life burns away in a great fire.

The Ender is a fictional character created by me for a Fantasy RPG with my friends, it has canine aspects and a very strong set of horns. This creature is considered a deity and has unknown and unlimited sources of power, their power is par with a neutronic star, meaning it could tore a void open in the middle of the universe and suck everything around it. Most religions and cults fear this "god", since every single time their appeared was to cause utmost destruction to everything and everyone. Almost nothing is known about their personality or how did they come into be, the few who were actually approached by them are offered deals of power and money, with very dangerous prices. For the RPG, The Ender is one of the last "bosses" to fight against the party, the group fo players have the choice to fight or not, since the quest resolves around finding and galaxy stone hidden away in the creature's lair. If the option of fighting is choosen by the party, the players must be over level 60 and have legendary equips, as well as buffs and lots of healing lapents. The fight is over when the players manage to trap the creature inside the galaxy stone, they cannot defeat it otherwise.

This creature is used to help the party deafeat the last boss of the campaign, The Void. Once inside the galaxy stone, the players can use some of the powers provided by The Ender. This is a unique way to reach the end, and can only be achieved by a lvl 60+ party. After the end of the campaign, the creature reveals it actually went along in being captured because it deemed them worthy of destroying The Void, it gives them all divine equips and new skills, opening a portal to somewhere and wishing everyone a good ending.

In the aftermath, once the summoner class player reaches lvl 100, it can summon a copy of this creature to use it's skills and help in battle. It has half the health of the player and 20 turns to use, this number may change according to the tablemaster.

ABILITIES

Phasing

Allows themselves to change their body atoms to pass through things and living beings, can also be used to avoid attacks.


Teleportation

This method allows them to travel great distances through aura concentration, they fast foward their body at light speed to change places. It cost a great deal of stamina.


Shape Shifting

They can change their body molecules to accomodate new shapes, big or smaller than their current size, the only drawback is that they cannot change color, making most of the new shapes unnatural and easily recognizable.


Elemental call-forthing

Can manipulate fire, ice and lightining as well as create atttacks based on this 3 elements, others elements are most just basic attacks.


Void Breaker

Most powerful attack, creates a concentrated sphere/beam of pure energy and launches at full force at the enemy, the results are very catastrophic. This attack is also used to create powerful wormholes that devour everything around.


Big Bangs

With a great concentration of energy, they can channel it to their horns and unleash a massive explosion, similar to a small big bang. This can also be applied to stars and other celest beings.
